# categorically eligible household

categorically eligible household A household considered eligible for SNAP benefits because all members receive or are authorized to receive TANF, GA, State Blind Pension, or SSI benefits.

A household considered eligible for SNAP Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits because all members receive or are authorized to receive TANF, GA, State Blind Pension, or SSI Supplemental Security Income. benefits.

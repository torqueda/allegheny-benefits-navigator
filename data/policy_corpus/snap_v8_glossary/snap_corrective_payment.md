# corrective payment

corrective payment An assistance payment made to a budget group that was underpaid in a previous month.

An assistance payment made to a budget group One or more related or unrelated individuals who occupy a common residence or would occupy a common residence if they were not homeless, and whose needs are considered together in determining eligibility for assistance are considered together in determining eligibility for assistance under one category of assistance. that was underpaid in a previous month.

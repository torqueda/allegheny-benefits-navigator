# head of household

head of household For SNAP, the adult member selected by the household to make the application, select a PIN for the EBT card, and make benefit transactions with the EBT card. The head of household is considered the principal wage earner.

For SNAP Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program , the adult A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training. member selected by the household to make the application, select a PIN Personal identification number. for the EBT Electronic Benefit Transfer. card, and make benefit transactions with the EBT card. The head of household is considered the principal wage earner.

# continuation of benefits

continuation of benefits The authorization of benefits in an unchanged amount if the budget group appeals within ten days of an adverse action notice.

The authorization of benefits in an unchanged amount if the budget group One or more related or unrelated individuals who occupy a common residence or would occupy a common residence if they were not homeless, and whose needs are considered together in determining eligibility for assistance are considered together in determining eligibility for assistance under one category of assistance. appeals within ten days of an adverse action notice.

# shelter for battered persons

shelter for battered persons A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

A public or private nonprofit residential facility that provides shelter and related services for individuals who have been physically abused by their spouses. The facility also provides shelter and services to the children of abused individuals residing in the facility.

# EBT card

EBT Electronic Benefit Transfer. card

A green,blue and yellow plastic card issued to persons who are authorized to receive Medical Assistance, SNAP Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program , or cash assistance benefits. The card allows recipients to electronically access their benefits.

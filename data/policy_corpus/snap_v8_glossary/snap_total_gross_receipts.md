# total gross receipts

total gross receipts A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

The amount received from self-employment before any deductions are given for the allowable costs of producing the self-employment income.

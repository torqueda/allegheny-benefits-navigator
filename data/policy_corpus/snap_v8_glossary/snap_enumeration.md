# enumeration

enumeration The process of verifying a person's Social Security Number (SSN) by requiring him to provide or apply for an SSN as a condition of eligibility.

The process of verifying a person's Social Security Number (SSN) An identifying number established for a person by the Social Security Administration. The number is used by DHS to verify identity in order to prevent duplicate participation in state and federal programs. The SSN is used to obtain income and resource information through computer matches. by requiring him to provide or apply for an SSN as a condition of eligibility.

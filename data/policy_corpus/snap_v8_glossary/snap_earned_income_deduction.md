# earned income deduction

earned income Cash or income in-kind received by a person in exchange for services. This includes wages, salaries, earnings from self-employment, training allowances, striker's earned income, and payments made under Title I of the Domestic Volunteer Service Act of 1973. deduction

A specific percentage subtracted from countable gross earned income to cover expenses associated with employment.

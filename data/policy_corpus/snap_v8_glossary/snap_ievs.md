# IEVS

IEVS Income Eligibility Verification System.

Income The money a household receives from any source. Eligibility Verification The use of written, visual, or oral information from someone other than the applicant or recipient to prove the accuracy of statements made by an applicant or recipient. System The Client Information System (CIS) and MAPPER system databases and processes. .

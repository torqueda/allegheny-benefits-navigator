# 368.5 Change of Category

When an applicant/recipient group becomes eligible for a different category of NMP , the CAO will apply the rules of the new category for which the group is eligible. Â The CAO must not require a new application for any of the following changes: Â Â

Â Â 55 Pa. Code Â§ 181.41

- From one NMP category to another

- From NMP to MNO

- From MNO to NMP

- From cash to NMP or MNO

Exception: Â A recipient of NMP who needs Long-Term Care (LTC) facility services must fill out a PA 600 L , even if LTC is authorized in an NMP category.

When changing categories, the CAO will:

- Apply the earned income incentive test to the earnings of an employed individual when approving PC, PU or PD; and

- Send the recipient an advance notice when changing the category from TANF-related to GA- related.

A change of category is effective the day after the end date of the advance notice period.

If a change in category leads to ineligibility for NMP, the CAO will close the NMP budget and determine eligibility for MNO.

Reminder: The CAO will consider whether an individual qualifies for Healthy Beginnings or Healthy Horizons .

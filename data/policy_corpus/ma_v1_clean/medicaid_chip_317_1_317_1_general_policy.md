The Breast and Cervical Cancer Prevention and Treatment (BCCPT) category , PH20, provides full Medical Assistance (MA) benefits to individuals with breast and cervical cancer, including precancerous conditions of the breast or cervix.

NOTE: Individuals with a diagnosis of ovarian or uterine cancer are NOT eligible for BCCPT.

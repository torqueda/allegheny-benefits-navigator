FMAP

Eligibility Rules

- No Medicare

- Age 0-64

- Non-disabled Childless adults Age 21-64 - 0-133% FPL

- Permanently Disabled adults Age 21-64, disability not verified by MRT/SSA - 0-133% FPL

- Non-Disabled Age 19-20 - above Enhanced MNO-133% FPL

- Disabled Age 19-20, disability verified by SSA/MRT - 102-133% FPL

- Disabled Age 19-20, disability not verified by SSA/MRT - above Enhanced MNO-133% FPL

- Women who become pregnant while open in MG91

- State/County Inmate Inpatient

- Permanent residents under the 5-year bar and temporary aliens

- Citizens, refugees, permanent residents over the 5-year bar

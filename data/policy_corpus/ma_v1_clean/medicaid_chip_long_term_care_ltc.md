# long-term care (LTC)

long-term care (LTC)

Services designed to provide diagnostic, therapeutic, rehabilitative, supportive or maintenance services for persons who have chronic functional impairments. Services may be provided in a variety of institutional and noninstitutional care settings, including the home.

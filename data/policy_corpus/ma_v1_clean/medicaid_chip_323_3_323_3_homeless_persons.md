# 323.3 Homeless Individuals

A homeless individual is considered to be a resident of Pennsylvania if the individual attests to be Â physically living in the state. A written statement, from someone who knows the homeless individual, saying that the individual is a resident , can be used as proof if it is questionable . The homeless individual does not need to have a permanent address or a fixed residence. Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â

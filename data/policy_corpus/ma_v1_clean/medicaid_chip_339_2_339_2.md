## 339.21 MG71 Eligibility - First Six Months

During the first six months of eligibility, individuals remain in MG71 if a child age 18 or under is in the MAGI household.

At the three month point in the certification, an alert (TMA 084) is created to prompt the worker to review the household in the fourth month to determine if the child is still in the household.

Example: Kelly and their child , Colin (age 13) are found eligible for MG71 on September 15th. Â The TMA 084 alert is generated in December. Â On December 19th, the CAO contacts Kelly and they report that Colin has moved in with their other parent full-time. Â MG71 for Kelly closes and they will be reviewed for other MA categories.

To qualify for continued or restored MA under the Pickle amendment, all of the following conditions must be met:

- The individual currently gets Social Security Title II RSDI benefits.

- The individual was entitled to Social Security Title II RSDI and SSI at the same time in any month after April 1977.

- SSI benefits were stopped after April 1977 due to receiving an RSDI COLA that caused them to no longer be financially eligible to receive the benefit

The individual received SSI while their RSDI was being calculated and then lost the SSI when the RSDI turned out to be higher than the SSI income limit at the time. Now, the individual芒聙聶s RSDI income (minus the COLAs received) is below the current SSI income limit.

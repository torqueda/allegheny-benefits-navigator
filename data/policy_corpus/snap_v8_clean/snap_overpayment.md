# overpayment

overpayment A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

The payment of LIHEAP funds or provision of LIHEAP benefits for which the agency or person is either fully or partially ineligible.

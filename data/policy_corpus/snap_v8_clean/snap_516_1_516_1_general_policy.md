# 516.1 General Policy

Eligibility requirements for households of migrant or seasonal farm workers are different from those of other households in the following areas:

- Application

- Expedited service

- Verification

- Income computation

- Work requirements

- Resources

- Income

- Semiannual reporting (SAR)

- Certification period

- Proration

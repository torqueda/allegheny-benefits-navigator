# 503.8 Strikes and Lockouts

People or organizations who are parties to a strike or lockout are not permitted to

- Certify applicant households,

- Interview households,

- Get proof needed from applicant households, or

- Use their facility as a site for any part of the certification process

These persons may be used as the source of verification if they would be the normal sources.

# 512.3 Issuing Benefits

The CAO will calculate net income for all categorically eligible and expanded categorically eligible households .

7 CFR Â§ 273.10 (e)(2)

For one- and two-person categorically eligible households, the CAO must issue a minimum $24 benefit, even if the Basis of Issuance Table shows no entitlement amount. (See Chapter 568, Appendix B . )

There is no minimum benefit for households with three or more persons. The CAO must issue the amount shown in the Basis of Issuance Tables.

NOTE: Categorically eligible households with three or more members may be entitled to benefits of at least $2. If the computation results in a benefit of $1, $3, or $5, round it up to $2, $4, or $6, respectively.

Exception: The benefit is prorated according to the proration table in the first application month. See Chapter 568, Appendix C .

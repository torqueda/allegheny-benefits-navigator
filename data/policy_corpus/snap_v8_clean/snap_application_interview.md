# application interview

application interview A face-to-face or telephone contact between an applicant and an eligibility worker for the purpose of establishing eligibility for benefits.

A face-to-face or telephone contact between an applicant A person who has submitted an application for himself or herself, or on behalf of another person, and whose eligibility has not been determined. and an eligibility worker for the purpose of establishing eligibility for benefits.

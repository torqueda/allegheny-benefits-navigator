# 505.5 Authorized Representative for a Resident of a Drug or Alcohol Treatment Center

The center must designate an employee to apply for and get SNAP benefits for each resident, including any children living with the resident.

7 CFR 273.11(e)(1)

To safeguard benefit security and accountability, the community agency should be a nonprofit agency that gives personal one-on-one help to clients. Area Agencies on Aging, mental health/mental retardation agencies, and homemaker agencies are examples.

Agencies requesting an AR designation must have the following:

- A written confidentiality policy that employees and volunteers agree to follow

- A bond to cover the persons who provide the service

- Management supervision

- Â Oversight of the service

The center will access benefits in the EBT account for food prepared by or served to its residents and comply with all requirements in Chapter 511 .

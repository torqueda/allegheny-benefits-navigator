# e-CIS

eCIS

The computer system The Client Information System (CIS) and MAPPER system databases and processes. that manages client A person who is applying for or getting benefits. Regulations that specify requirements for a "client" apply to both applicants and recipients. information related to applications, payment An approved cash or crisis benefit issued through the LIHEAP automated system. issuances, and report generation.

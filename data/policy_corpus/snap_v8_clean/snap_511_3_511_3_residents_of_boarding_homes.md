# 511.3 Residents of Boarding Homes

A commercial boarding home is a business licensed to provide meals and lodging for payment. In areas that do not require a license, a boarding home is defined as a business that provides meals and lodging with the intent to make a profit.

Residents of boarding homes are not eligible to participate in SNAP unless the boarding home qualifies as a GLA . (See Section 511.7 .)

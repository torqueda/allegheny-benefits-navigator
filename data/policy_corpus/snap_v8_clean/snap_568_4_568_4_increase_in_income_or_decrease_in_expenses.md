There is no overpayment as long as the household has reported the change timely and the CAO has acted timely.

Examples:

- A non- SAR household reports a $75 increase in unearned income on June 23. The Advance Notice of Adverse Action expires on July 6. Regardless of the issuance cycle, the CAO must decrease the household's benefits for August.

- A non-SAR household reports a $75 increase in unearned income on June 17. The Advance Notice of Adverse Action expires on June 30. Regardless of the issuance cycle, the CAO must decrease the household's benefits for July.

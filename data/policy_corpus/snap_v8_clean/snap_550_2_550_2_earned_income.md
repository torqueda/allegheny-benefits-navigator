# 550.2 Earned Income

The County Assistance Office ( CAO) must use the gross amount of earned income with no deductions for taxes. Earned income includes the following: 脗

- Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)-funded subsidized employment income, regardless of the source of TANF funding (e.g., Emergency Fund, TANF block grant, or State Maintenance of Effort (MOE) requirement ).

- Sick benefits paid as wages by an employer to an employee who expects to return to work.

# uninterrupted benefits

uninterrupted benefits A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

A household's receipt of its SNAP benefits on the regular issuance date each month, with no delay in benefits.

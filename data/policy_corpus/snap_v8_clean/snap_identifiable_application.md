# identifiable application

identifiable application For SNAP, an application form containing the name and address of the applicant and the signature of a responsible member of the household or an authorized representative.

For SNAP Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program , an application form containing the name and address of the applicant A person who has submitted an application for himself or herself, or on behalf of another person, and whose eligibility has not been determined. and the signature of a responsible member of the household A person or group of persons applying for or receiving SNAP benefits. or an authorized representative.

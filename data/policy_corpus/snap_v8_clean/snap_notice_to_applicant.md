# Notice to Applicant

Notice to Applicant A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

The notification form (PA/FS 162) used to tell the client about a decision on an application or request for benefits.

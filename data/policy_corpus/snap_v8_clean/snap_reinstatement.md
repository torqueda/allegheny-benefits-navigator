# reinstatement

reinstatement A person who is 19 years of age or older or who is 18 years of age and not a full-time student in a secondary school or in the equivalent level of vocational or technical training.

The process by which the CAO reactivates benefits for a household whose benefits have been suspended because of its failure to comply with monthly reporting requirements.

# 516.10 Proration

Migrant or seasonal farm worker households are eligible to receive a full monthly benefit for the application month if the household has participated in Pennsylvania or another state in the 30 days before the application month.

The CAO must data-enter an open date equal to the first day of the application month. The system accepts an opening transaction only if the âapplication received dateâ on the PA 601 is on or before the open date.

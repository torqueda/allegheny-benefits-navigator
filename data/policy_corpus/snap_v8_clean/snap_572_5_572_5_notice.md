# 572.5 Notice

The CAO must send a Confirming Notice ( PA/FS 162-C ) to notify the household of a mass change in the cash grant or federal benefits.

The CAO must take the following actions:

- Send a PA/FS 162-C to SNAP-only households when increasing, decreasing, or closing benefits during the certification period .

- Send a PA/FS 162-C to combination cash assistance/SNAP or Medical Assistance (MA)/SNAP households when a PA/FS 162-A is not required to decrease or close cash assistance or MA benefits.

- Send the PA/FS 162-C so that the household receives it no later than the day the household receives the benefit. This applies to all households, regardless of how they are to report changes.

- If the change in benefits is made at renewal , send a Notice to Applicant ( PA/FS 162 ). The household must receive the notice no later than the end of the current certification period.

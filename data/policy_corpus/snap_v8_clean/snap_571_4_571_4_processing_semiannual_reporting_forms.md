## 571.41 Complete SAR Form Received by Due Date

The CAO will review the SAR form. If it is complete and received by the due date, the CAO will: Â

## 571.42 Complete SAR Form Received after Due Date

When the CAO receives a SAR form after the SAR form due date on which all questions are answered completely and required verification is attached, the CAO will:

- Track the SAR form as complete if it is received by the last day to track it and time permits tracking.

- Reactivate, suspend, or terminate benefits.

- Promptly mail a confirming notice to tell the household of any action taken, such as suspension, reinstatement , termination, or change. The notice must include the reason for the action and be based on information reported on the SAR form.

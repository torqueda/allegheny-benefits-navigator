# 581.5 Client Notice

Whether the CAO discovers an error or the household asks for restored benefits , the CAO must notify the household of the following:

- The household's entitlement to lost benefits

- The amount of benefits to be restored

- The amount of any overissuance being offset

- The method of restoration

- The household's right to appeal

The CAO must send a notice of ineligibility if requested benefits are denied.

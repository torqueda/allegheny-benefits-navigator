# 603.2 Program Schedule

LIHEAP generally runs from November through March of each program year, but it may be extended or shortened, depending on available funding. DHS publishes the exact startup and closing dates in the State Plan. The State Plan is amended when program dates are changed.

Current season dates are published in Chapter 603, Appendix A .

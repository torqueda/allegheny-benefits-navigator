# 603.5 Privacy

Information about a LIHEAP applicant or recipient is confidential and may be given out only for the following reasons:

- To help in the investigation or prosecution of suspected fraud in connection with LIHEAP.

- To help federal or state officials with LIHEAP audits, reviews and investigations.

- To help the household apply for or receive other forms of energy assistance as long as the applicant or recipients agree to disclosure .

- To allow DHS to obtain a record of the costs of shelter, heating and heating use. Â

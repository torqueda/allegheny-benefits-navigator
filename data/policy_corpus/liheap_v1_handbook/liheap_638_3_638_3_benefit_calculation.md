# 638.3 Benefit Calculation

The LIHEAP benefit is calculated using an eligible household's size, income, primary fuel type, and heating region.

Household Size-

Household Size values range from 1-20.

Income-

The household's income, after all allowable exclusions and deductions, is categorized by the bracket in which it falls. Brackets begin at $0 and increase in $1,000 increments:

- [$0-$999]

- [$1,000-$1,999]

- [$2,000- $2,999]

- etc.

Each bracket is associated with a different benefit multiplier. the lowest income brackets are associated with higher multipliers.

Fuel Type-

Fuel types include:

701- Electric

702- Fuel Oil

703- Coal

704- Natural Gas

705- Kerosene

706- Propane or Bottled Gas

707- Wood/Other

708- Blended Fuel (defined strictly as a 50/50 blend of fuel oil and kerosene)

The values associated with each fuel type depend on the cost of the fuel in the household's county of residence. these values are subject to change each program year.

NOTE: Benefit amount is calculated using primary fuel type, even if the household is requesting payment go to their secondary fuel source.

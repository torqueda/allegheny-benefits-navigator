# 522_Appendix C

Use this information sheet to determine whether an individual has sufficient earnings to yield a quarter of coverage.

## For Quarters Prior to 1978

- A credit was earned for each calendar quarter in which an individual was paid $50 or more in wages (including agriculture wages for 1951â1955).

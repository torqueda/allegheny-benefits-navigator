# 516.5 Work Requirements

A migrant or seasonal farm worker is exempt from work requirements if he or she is under contract to, or has an agreement with, an employer or crew chief to begin employment within 30 days. The CAO must assign employment/training exemption code 21 to migrant or seasonal farm workers who meet this exemption. Â

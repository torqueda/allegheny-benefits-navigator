The household is notified of the zero issuance for August and the continuation of benefits beginning in September. The CAO must remove the income for September.

## 571.81 Processing one Month of Zero Issuance or a Temporary Increase in Income

When a household submits a SAR form showing it received income that is not expected to last, the CAO will average only the income that is expected to continue during the semiannual period.

Example: A household submits a SAR form in January and provides all pay stubs for December. The person got overtime pay in the holiday season. The overtime is not expected to continue. The CAO will not average the overtime pay when determining the income to adjust for the next SAR period.

## 571.82 Termination of Benefits

The CAO will terminate a household's benefits if the information shown on the SAR form or L/IN shows ineligibility that is expected to last for more than one month. The CAO will send a Confirming Notice to the household.

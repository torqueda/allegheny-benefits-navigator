# 506.5 Denials

The County Assitance Office ( CAO) must follow these steps on the day it decides that a household is not entitled to expedited service :

- Tell the household that the expedited service is denied and give or send a Notice of Denial of Expedited Service ( PA/FS 163 ).

- Tell the household that it has the right to meet with a supervisor within two work days.

- Mail the ( PA/FS 163 ) to the household when the household is interviewedÂ by telephone. This notice does not take the place of the Notice of Ineligibility ( PA/FS 162 ) telling applicants they are not eligible for Supplemantal Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) benefits.

- Screen the application and schedule an interview using normal application processing procedures.

- Add the following information to the case record:

- Why expedited service was denied

- How the household was told of the denial

- How the household was told of its right to an agency conference

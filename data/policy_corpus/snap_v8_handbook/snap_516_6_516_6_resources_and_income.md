# 516.6 Resources and Income

The resource rules in Chapter 540 and income rules in Chapters 550 , 552 , and 555 apply to migrant or seasonal farm worker households. The CAO must count income that is and will be available to the household for the certification period . The following special rules also apply:

- The CAO must exempt the value of a licensed vehicle used for long distance travel while the migrant or seasonal farm worker follows the job stream. Â

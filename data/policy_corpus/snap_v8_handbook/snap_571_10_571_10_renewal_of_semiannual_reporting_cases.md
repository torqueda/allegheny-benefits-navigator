# 571.10 Renewal of Semiannual Reporting Cases

A household required to report semiannually must be assigned a certification period effective the first month of certification, including any expedited issuance. (See Chapter 575 for determining the length of the certification period . )

The CAO Â must renew the household before the SAR form due date in the 12th month. The renewal Â interview must include the requirements in Chapter 576 .

To coordinate the SAR process with the renewal process, the CAO will:

- Send the PA 10-SP form to the household so that it arrives before the end of the month before the last month of the certification period.

- Attempt to schedule the renewal interview before the SAR form due date in the last month of the certification period.

- Enter the renewal before the regular SNAP issuance deadline to continue benefits.

The individual completing the application must verify their identity. If an AR applies for the household , the CAO must verify the identity of both the AR and the payment name.

Identity may be verified by readily available documents or by collateral contact . The CAO must accept any document that reasonably proves identity. No specific type of document, such as a birth certificate, is required.

NOTE: The CAO will enter into eCIS the gender that is specified on identity verification.

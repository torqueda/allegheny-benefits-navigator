# 679.2 Reconsideration

A new application (paper or electronic) is not required when an application was denied and the individual requests the application to be reconsidered, asks for a new application, and/or submits required verification within 60 days of the application date. Reconsiderations may also be completed as part of the appeal process.

Exception: If it discovered that the CAO incurred an administrative error when processing the application, reconsideration may be processed outside of the 60-day timeframe. Administrative error may include but is not limited to failure to review information available in imaging/IEVS, failure to add/remove an individual prior to processing, and data entry of incorrect income of living arrangement.

# 677.4 Incomplete Notice

When an application is incomplete, DHS must send the applicant a notice with the following information:

- The name of the applicant.

- The information needed to complete the application.

- The date the pending information is needed.

When an incomplete application is data-entered into the system, an incomplete notice is generated automatically and is mailed to the applicant. Incomplete notices are generated daily.

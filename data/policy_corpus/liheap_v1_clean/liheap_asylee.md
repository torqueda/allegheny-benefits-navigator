# asylee

asylee

A person seeking political asylum, the status granted by the USCIS to an alien living in the U.S. because of a well-founded fear of persecution in the personâs country because of his or her race, religion, ethnic group, or social group. This status is similar to refugee status; the difference is that refugees are granted their status abroad while persons seeking asylum apply after they enter the U.S.

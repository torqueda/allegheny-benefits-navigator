# United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)

United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)

The agency in the U.S. Department of Homeland Security that is responsible for the administration and enforcement of the U.S. immigration laws. These services were formerly provided by the Immigration and Naturalization Service (INS).

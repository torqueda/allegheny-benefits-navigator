# 379.2 Reopening Closed Benefits

A new application (paper or electronic) is not required when benefits are closed and the individual requests that MA benefits be reopened, asks for a new application, and/or submits required documents within 60 days of the date MA benefits were closed. See Section 304.16, Where to Find a Form, and Who Does or Does Not Need One .

Example: Â MA budget closed on March 15, 20xx and the appropriate ineligibility notice was sent. Â Request for reconsideration is received within 60 days of the MA closure date but no later than May 13, 20xx.

When an individual requests that benefits be reopened and/or submits required documents and it is within 60 days of benefits closure, reconsider the case using the paperwork and information in the case record on the date benefits were closed plus any paperwork or information the individual submitted after benefits were closed.

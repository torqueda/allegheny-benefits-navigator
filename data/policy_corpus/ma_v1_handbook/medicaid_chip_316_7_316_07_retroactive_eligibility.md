# 316.7 Retroactive Eligibility

The CAO will determine who gets retroactive coverage for MAWD based on the requirements in this section and the following ones:

- Section 338.3, Retroactive Medical Assistance Eligibility

- Section 338.3, Retroactive Authorization

The CAO must use the following guidelines to determine who is eligible for MAWD and what the monthly premium payment should be:

- The MAWD applicant must meet all of the MAWD requirements, including the work requirement for retroactive coverage.

- The premium amount for the retroactive coverage is set by using the actual income for the months in which the coverage is asked for. ( Section 316.61, Premium Determinations .)

The CAO will let the applicant know that retroactive coverage will not be granted until they pay the premiums due for the retroactive coverage period. The CAO will also let the individual know that the application for retroactive coverage will be held for 30 days. The 30-day count begins on the date the decision on retroactive eligibility is made.

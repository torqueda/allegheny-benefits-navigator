# 312.7 Former Foster Care

Individuals who were in foster care and enrolled in MA at any time on or after their 18th birthday may qualify for MA in a special category. Â Â The category/program status code PC 40 is assigned to the individuals in this group.

The individuals eligible for MA coverage under the Former Foster Care category are those who:

- Are under age 26.

- Are not currently eligible or enrolled in a MAGI-related MA category mandated by federal regulation or SSI-related Non-Money Payment (NMP) MA

- MG90 and MG91 are optional expansion categories that are not mandated by federal regulations. Â PC40 must be authorized prior to either of these categories.

- MG27, MG 23, MG 71, MG00, MG19 are MAGI-related MA mandatory categories .

Exception: Â Medically Needy Only (MNO) MA and MNO spend down should not be authorized for former foster care recipients. PC40 must be authorized if the individual is not eligible for mandatory MAGI MA (MG27, MG 23, MG 71, MG00, MG19) or SSI-related NMP.

- Received MA in federal or state-funded foster care on or after their 18 th birthday. Â

## 391.41 Effective Date of Eligibility for the Supplement

For the effective date on the PA 747 , the CAO must use the date the AAA or PCH completes the application (Section 3 of the PA 761 ) .

The SSA sets the effective date that the supplement must be paid by using the date the CAO enters on the PA 747 and based on the following rules:

- If the individual is getting SSI, the effective date is the first day of the month in which the application is completed by the PCH or AAA.

- If the individual is approved for SSI but is not yet getting SSI benefits, the effective date is one of the following:

- The date SSI began if the Â PA 761 or PA 1-D is dated after the SSI begin date and in the same month as the SSI begin date.

- The date SSI began if the PA 761 or PA 1-D is dated in a month prior to the SSI begin date.

- The first day of the calendar month in whichÂ the PA 761 or PA 1-D is dated if it is dated in a calendar month following the month SSI began.

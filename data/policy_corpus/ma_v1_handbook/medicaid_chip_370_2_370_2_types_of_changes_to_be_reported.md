# 370.2 Types of Changes to be Reported

Individuals will report the following changes: Â Â Â Â Â Â

- A new mailing address .

- A change or expected change in residence (including plans to leave Pennsylvania or the county, even if the individual plans to return).

- A change in U.S. citizen or immigration status (see Chapter 378, Verification ).

- Individuals moving in or out of the home (including friends, a newborn child, or other relatives).

- A change in marital status (including separation, divorce, remarriage, annulment, or common-law marriage) of anyone living in the home.

- A change in earned income or unearned income , including lump sums.

Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Note : Â This change must be reported by the tenth day of the month after the individual receives the income.

- A change in income-expense deductions.

- A change in expected medical expenses that were used for eligibility.

- The purchase or sale of real property or personal property (including tools, equipment and stock).

- Bank accounts, life insurance, vehicles or other resources bought, sold, traded or converted.

- A change in student status, if the category or income exemptions would be affected.

- A new job or a change of job.

- A pregnancy or the end of a pregnancy.

- A change in medical insurance coverage or other third party liability.

- A change in a condition that was used to consider an individual disabled (for example, an improvement in a medical condition).

- A change that requires the CAO to allow good cause for stopping MAWD monthly premium payments (for example, a change in or loss of employment. or a health problem).

- Any other changes that may affect eligibility.

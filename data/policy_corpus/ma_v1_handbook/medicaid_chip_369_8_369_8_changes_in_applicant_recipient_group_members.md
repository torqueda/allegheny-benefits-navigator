## 369.82 Removals from the Applicant/Recipient Group

A member must be removed from the applicant/recipient group when he or she:

- Leaves the household.

- No longer meets eligibility or category requirements.

- Becomes eligible for a different category or type of MA.

A member can also be removed when doing so would make the remaining members eligible for benefits.

When an individual is removed from an applicant/recipient group, the CAO will take the following actions:

- Figure out MNO eligibility for a new six-month period beginning the month the change took place. Use the rules of Section 369.4, Continuing MNO , as follows:

- Include the income the deleted member actually got while they were in the home.

- If the applicant/recipient group is ineligible with the individual removed, close the budget.

- If the applicant/recipient group is eligible with the individual removed, delete them. Continue MNO for the remaining members.

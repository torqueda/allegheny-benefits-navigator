# 305.6 Other Categories

Additional coverage groups that have criteria different than SSI-related, GA-related, TANF-related, and MAGI-related MA are:

- Breast and Cervical Cancer Prevention and Treatment (BCCPT)

- Children with Special Needs

- Foster Care and Adoption

- Refugee Medical Assistance

- Incarcerated Individuals

Former Foster Care

Children in substitute care placement by a County Children and Youth Agency (CCYA) or who were adopted out of a substitute care placement by a CCYA are assigned category PC . If the child is also eligible for Long-Term Care, they are assigned category PCN . The PSC is assigned based on the type of placement and the funding source.

Children who are released from a Youth Detention Center (YDC) or Youth Forestry Camp (YFC) may be eligible for a limited period upon release from these facilities. They are assigned category PC with PSC 30.

See Chapter 850, Foster Care and Adoption for more information.

Children identified as Unaccompanied Refugee Minors are assigned category PC and PSC 03 . See Section 730.22, Unaccompanied Refugee Minors for more information.

Individuals who are eligible and receive MA in a foster care-related category on or after their 18 th birthday are eligible to receive MA until the age of 26 in the former foster care category. They are assigned category PC and PSC 40 . There is no income or resource test for this group. Incarceration - Suspended

When individuals are incarcerated in a correctional institution, they may remain open in MA for the duration of their incarceration. They are assigned category MG and PSC 99 . Individuals enrolled in MG 99 are in a suspended status and are not eligible to receive full MA benefits.

If an incarcerated individual has an inpatient medical need, the correctional institution will complete an application on their behalf and the individual will be assigned to a category based on their non-financial criteria:

- SSI-Related: PH

- TANF-Related: PC

- GA-Related: PD

- MAGI-Related: MG

PSC is assigned based on the type of correctional facility:

- State Correctional Institution (SCI): 38

- County Correctional Institutions: 39

See Section 392, Correctional Institutions for more information.

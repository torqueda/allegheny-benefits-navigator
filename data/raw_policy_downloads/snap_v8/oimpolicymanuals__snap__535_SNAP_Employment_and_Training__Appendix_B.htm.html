CAO-Directed Project (Project Code: SA)

The CAO will only refer a SNAP E&T participant to a CAO-directed project if the participant is an ABAWD seeking to meet their work requirement through community service and does not wish to be referred to SNAP EARN.Â Â The CAO would track participation in the SA Project by prospectively verifying hours through completion of the PA 1938 Community Service Verification Form, for up to six months in advance.Â Â If the participant secures employment while participating in community service, the participant may also participate in the SNAP Job Retention (AC-93) activity for 90 days under the CAO-directed project.

SPALs are available to participants when needed and required to support participation.

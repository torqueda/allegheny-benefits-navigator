506.82 Issuing Benefits for Households Applying after the 15th of the Month

If the household provides all required verification within the 5 -day time limit and is eligible, the CAO must process a regular opening with a centrally issued OTI for the first and second months. Â Â 

If verification is postponed, the CAO must process a locally issued NCE for the first and second months.

  • Determine eligibility for continued benefits; and

  • Process a regular opening for the third month, if the household is eligible.

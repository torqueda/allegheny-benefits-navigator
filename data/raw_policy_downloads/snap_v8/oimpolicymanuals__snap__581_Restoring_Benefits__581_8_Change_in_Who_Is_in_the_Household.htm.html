581.8 Change in Who Is in the Household

If the household changes, the CAO must issue restored benefits to the household with the majority of persons who were household members when the loss happened.

If the CAO cannot locate the household or cannot determine which household contains the majority, the CAO must restore benefits to the household that contains the person who was the payment name when the loss occurred.

If the CAO does not know where the payment name and the majority are, it must restore benefits to the household with the greater number of remaining members, even if it is only one member.

567.3 At the SAR Review or at Renewal

The County Assistance Office (CAO) must use the best estimate of income reported on the "Semiannual Reporting Form" (PA 564)Â or the "Benefits Review" (PA 600R) Â Â renewal application, in addition to any known or reported changes, to determine the benefit amount for the next SAR or certification period.

NOTE: The client must report all income on the PA 564 or PA 600R. If verification of all income expected and received in the semiannual reporting (SAR) or renewal month is not submitted, the form should not be considered complete.

Exception: If the CAO can determine the actual amount received in the reporting month, the SAR or renewal form may be considered complete. For example, an individual paid weekly provides their first, second, and fourth pay stubs of the month. The CAO can use the year-to-date on the fourth pay stub to determine the actual amount received in the reporting month.

The CAO must follow the policy in Section 567.2 for household income that is expected to continue into the next SAR or certification period.

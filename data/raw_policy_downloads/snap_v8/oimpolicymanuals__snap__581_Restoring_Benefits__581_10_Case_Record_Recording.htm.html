581.10 Case Record Recording

When the CAO issues restored benefits, the case record must contain the following information:

  • The reason for restoring lost benefits

  • The computation of the amount of restored benefits

  • The balance of benefits to be restored to the household when the household asks to have benefits restored over several months

  • A copy of the overissuance claim if one is used to reduce the amount of restored benefits

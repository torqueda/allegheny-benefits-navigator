571.4 Processing Semiannual Reporting Forms

If the CAO tracks the SAR form as complete, benefits will continue.

If the CAO tracks the SAR form as incomplete or does not track it at all, the system generates a SAR L/IN and suspends the case.

571.41 Complete SAR Form Received by Due Date

The CAO will review the SAR form. If it is complete and received by the due date, the CAO will: Â

  • Track the SAR form as complete by the last day to track it.

  • Determine continued eligibility and benefit amount using the information on the SAR form.

  • Contact the household and record the contact results in the case record if the SAR form is complete but it shows a change that will affect benefits and verification of the change is not attached.

  • Contact the household and record the contact results in the case record if the SAR form is complete but the information is incorrect or inconsistent with information provided or known by the CAO.

  • Mail a Confirming Notice to tell the household of any action taken, such as suspension, termination, or change in the benefit. The notice must include the reason for the action and be based on information reported on the SAR form. The household must receive the Confirming Notice no later than the next regular issuance date.

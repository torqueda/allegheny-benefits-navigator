581.7 Method of Restoration

The CAO usually authorizes restored benefits in full. This authorization is in addition to the regular monthly benefit issued to a SNAP household. The CAO must use reason code 158 when authorizing restored benefits.

The CAO must honor a reasonable request by a household to restore lost benefits in monthly installments.

Example: A household asks for restored benefits in monthly installments, because the amount restored is more than the household can use in a reasonable period of time.

505.6 Authorized Representative for a Resident of a Group Living Arrangement

A resident of a group living arrangement (GLA) may apply:

  • As a one-person household;

  • Through an AR of his or her choice; or

  • As a one-person household using a GLA-designated employee as the AR.

The GLA must decide if a resident may apply on his or her own. The GLA must first consult with any other state agencies that provide services to the resident and then decide whether the resident is physically and mentally able to handle his or her own affairs.Â 

Not all residents of the GLA have to be certified through an AR or individually for one or the other methods to be used. The GLA may choose a staff member to be the AR or have the GLA act as an AR. See Section 505.5.

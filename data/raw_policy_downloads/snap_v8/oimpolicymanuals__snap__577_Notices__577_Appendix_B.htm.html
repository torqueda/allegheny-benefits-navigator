CM 430 explains reporting requirements for households enrolled in semiannual reporting (SAR). SAR households are required to report when the gross monthly income exceeds 130% of the federal poverty income guidelines (FPIG) amount. The message contains a chart that displays the 130% FPIG amount for various household sizes. Each October, the United States Department of Agriculture mandates a change to the FPIG. CM 430 is mailed annually to SNAP-only and SNAP/Medical Assistance (MA) households enrolled in SAR. CM 430 is available in English and Spanish.

  • Travel time is two or more hours round-trip from an employment/education training site (ETP Code 19)

The following individuals who previously received a discretionary exemption will now receive a federal exemption instead:

  • Full-Time AmeriCorps VISTA volunteers will now be coded ETP Code 17 and Qual Code 3 and will receive a federal exemption due to working at least 30 hours per week or earning a stipend (in-kind pay) equal to 30 times the minimum wage per week.
  • Expedited SNAP recipients who have used less than three countable months and have verification of an exemption or work requirement pending at the time expedited benefits are issued will now be coded ETP Code 23 and Qual Code 16 and will receive a federal exemption for the expedited period.

The following individuals who previously received a discretionary exemption will now be subject to the time limit:

  • Expedited SNAP recipients who have already established they meet no exemption and are not meeting the work requirement at the time expedited benefits are issued will be coded ETP Code 30 and Qual Code 20 and will incur a countable month if their expedited NCE contains a full calendar month.

NOTE: Individuals who apply for SNAP on the 2nd through 15th days of the month will not incur a countable month in the month of application because their expedited benefit only covers a partial month.

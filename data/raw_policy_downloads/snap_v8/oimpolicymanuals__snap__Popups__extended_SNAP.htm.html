Extended SNAP Benefits provided to all households leaving TANF (except those prohibited by regulation). SNAP benefits are frozen for five months to provide continued support of the nutritional needs of families as they move from TANF to self-sufficiency. The amount of Extended SNAP benefits is determined by subtracting the TANF grant amount from the household income received in the last month of TANF eligibility.

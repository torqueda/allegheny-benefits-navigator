571.7 Failure to Submit a Semiannual Reporting Form or a Complete Late/Incomplete Notice by the Extended Due Date

The CFSAR system will suspend benefits for any household that does not submit either a SAR form or complete L/IN by the extended due date, no notice is required.

If there is no response to the L/IN, the CAO will record in the case record that the case is suspended. Â 

  • Do not send notification of termination of benefits as the L/IN serves as the notice of closing.

  • If the case is reinstated due to good cause, the CAO will record the good-cause reason in the case record.

  • If the case is later reinstated after the CFSAR update deadline, the CAO will enter the correct CFSAR code to reenroll the case in the CFSAR system.

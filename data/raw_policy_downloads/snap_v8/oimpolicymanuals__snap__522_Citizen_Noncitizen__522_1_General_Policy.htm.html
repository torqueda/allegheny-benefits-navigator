Non-Citizen Immigration Status, PFS18950522 (Published Â May 29, 2018)

Participants in the SNAP program must be one of the following:

  • U.S citizen

  • A non-citizen in a category that makes the individual eligible for SNAP benefits on the same basis a a citizen, including U.S. non-citizen nationals from American Samoa and Swains Island

  • An individual who is determined to be a qualified non-citizen based on the status assigned by the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) and meets a condition which allows the individual to receive SNAP benefits either indefinitely or for a limited period of time.

An applicant for SNAP benefits must attest in writing on the PA 600, Application for Benefits, that each individual for whom he or she is applying is a U.S. citizen or non-citizen in lawful immigration status.

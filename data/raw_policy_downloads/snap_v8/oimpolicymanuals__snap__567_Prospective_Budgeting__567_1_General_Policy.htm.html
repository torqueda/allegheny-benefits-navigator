567.1 General Policy

Determining eligibility for the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) is a two-step process:

  1. Determine if the household meets the program rules for SNAP;

  2. If yes to #1, determine the benefit amount. (see Chapter 568, Computing Eligibility and Benefit Amount).

Prospective budgeting (PB) is used to determine the benefit amount for all SNAP households.

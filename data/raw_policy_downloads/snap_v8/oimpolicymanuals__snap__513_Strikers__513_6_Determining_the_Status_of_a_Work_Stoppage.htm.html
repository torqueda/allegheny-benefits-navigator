513.6 Determining the Status of a Work Stoppage

If the company and the union disagree about whether a work stoppage is a strike or a lockout, the CAO will provide its area manager with the following information:

  • The name and address of the company

  • The names and telephone numbers of company and union representatives

  • The date the work stoppage began

  • Demographic information about an applicant

The area manager will forward this information to the Bureau of Policy.

The Bureau of Policy will:

  • Contact the company and union representatives for their statements, and

  • Contact the PA Department of Labor and Industry to ask whether it has issued an official determination and if unemployment compensation benefits are authorized.

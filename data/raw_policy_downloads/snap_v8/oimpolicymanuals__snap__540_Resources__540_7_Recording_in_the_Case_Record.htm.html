540.7 Recording in the Case Record

Case files must be documented to support eligibility, ineligibility, and benefit level determinations. Documentation will be in sufficient detail to permit a reviewer to determine the rationale and accuracy of the determination.

The County Assistance Office (CAO) will include the following entries in the case record:

  • The value of all countable resources in the case record

  • Show calculations for determining equity value or the value of resources with allowable deductions and exclusions

  • The total value of resources used to determine if the household is within the resource limit and the limit used

  • The reason for the action taken

  • Any individuals disqualified or sanctioned

  • Any unusual circumstances

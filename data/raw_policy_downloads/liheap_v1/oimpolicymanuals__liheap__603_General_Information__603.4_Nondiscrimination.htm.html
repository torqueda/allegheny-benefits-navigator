603.4 Nondiscrimination

No one can be excluded from participating in LIHEAP, denied LIHEAP benefits or be subject to discrimination in a project or activity funded by LIHEAP because of their race, color, sex, age, disability, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, ancestry or political belief.

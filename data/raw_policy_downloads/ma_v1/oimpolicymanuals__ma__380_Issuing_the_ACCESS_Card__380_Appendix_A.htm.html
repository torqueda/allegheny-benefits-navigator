Description Of ACCESS Card

The ACCESS card is a standard size plastic card with a yellow background and blue lettering or a white card with black lettering.

Front of ACCESS Card

ACCESS

Name of card and Keystone logo.

RECIP or ID#

Nine-digit eCIS Individual Number and one-digit validation number now used by medical providers and other authorized providers such as MA Transportation providers to identify individual eligibility and to bill the Department for services.

NAME:

Name of individual as displayed on eCIS Individual Inquiry Screen.

CARD ISSUE #

Number assigned to each card for security purposes by the Eligibility Verification System (EVS). This number is randomly assigned and not in sequential order. The card issue number is voided by issuance of a replacement card.

NOTE: Â Medical providers receive message "individual not eligible" when a voided ACCESS card is used. Remind individuals to destroy voided cards.

Back of ACCESS Card

MAGNETIC STRIPE

(yellow card only)

Coding stripe that contains information used by point of sale (POS) devices to identify eligibility for medical benefits.

SIGNATURE PANEL

(yellow card only)

The blue signature stripe is tamper proof. Individuals should not erase signature. Individuals should sign card for their own protection. Recipients age 18 or over, or under 18 and head of household should sign card. Children may sign if they wish.

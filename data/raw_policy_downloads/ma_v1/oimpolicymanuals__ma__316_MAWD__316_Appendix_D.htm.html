Retroactive Benefits

If the applicant requests and is authorized retroactive MAWD benefits, the initial premium statement issued by the CAO must include the premium amount for each retroactive month.

Â 

Reminder: Data entry of a retroactive MAWD eligibility period cannot be completed until the Central Office notifies the CAO by Alert #182 that the retroactive premium(s) has been paid. If the CAO is waiting for notification of payment of the premium for retroactive months, do not delay authorization of ongoing MAWD benefits.

Ongoing Benefits

If the data entry (for ongoing benefits) is completed after the eligibility begin date, include the monthly premium for each ongoing month beginning with the first month of ongoing eligibility and ending with the current month on the initial premium statement.

Retroactive and ongoing monthly premiums are included on the same initial premium statement.

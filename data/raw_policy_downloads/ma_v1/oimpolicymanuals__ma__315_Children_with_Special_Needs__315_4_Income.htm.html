315.41 Excluded Income

The following income is excluded when determining eligibility for the Children with Special Needs category:

  • RSDI payments

  • Court-ordered child support payments

    • As verified in the Pennsylvania Child Support Enforcement System (PACSES), in determining MA eligibility for a child with a disability. Court-ordered child support payments from out of state, which may not be in PACSES, will also be excluded if documentation is provided. To apply this disregard, the child must have acceptable verification of a disability which meets the SSA disability criteria (see Section 315.2). If the child has not been determined disabled by either SSA or DHS's MRT, then a DAP referral should be completed for MRT review.

378.2 Applicant/Recipient Responsibility

If the CAO is unable to verify an individual's statement through electronic data sources or the verification obtained electronically is not reasonably compatible with the individual's statement then the individual is responsible for proving his or her statements. If the proof the individual provides is not good enough, not complete, or not consistent, they must cooperate with the CAO in getting other proof.

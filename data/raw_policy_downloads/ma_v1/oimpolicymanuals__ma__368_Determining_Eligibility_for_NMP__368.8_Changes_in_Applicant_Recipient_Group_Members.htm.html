When a client asks to add another individual or the CAO says that an individual who is applying for NMP must be included in an applicant/recipient group that already exists, the CAO will treat the individual as an applicant. (See Chapter 304, Application.)

368.82 Removals from the Applicant/Recipient Group

A member must be removed from the applicant/recipient group when he or she leaves the household or no longer meets eligibility or category requirements. A member can also be removed when doing so would make the remaining members eligible for benefits.

When an individual is removed from an applicant/recipient group, the CAO must take the following actions:

  • Determine NMP eligibility again for the other members, using the rules in Section 368.3, Continuing NMP, and Section 368.4, NMP Spend-Down.

  • If the applicant/recipient group is ineligible with the individual removed, approve closure effective the day after the advance notice period ends.

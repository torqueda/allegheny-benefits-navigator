Appendix A: Determining Category Using Information Provided on the PA 1663, PA 1664, and PA 1671

The CAO will use DHS's Employability Assessment Form (PA 1663), the Temporary Disability Reassessment Form (PA 1664), or the Health-Sustaining Medication Assessment Form (PA 1671) to determine MA eligibility.

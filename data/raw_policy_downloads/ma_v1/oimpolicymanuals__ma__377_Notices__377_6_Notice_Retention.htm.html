377.6 Notice Retention

The CAO copy of all notices sent to an applicant or recipient will be kept for six years, either in the CAO case record or in electronic documents.

Â 

If an appeal is filed, the notice will be kept until the appeal is resolved or until the end of the six years, whichever is later.

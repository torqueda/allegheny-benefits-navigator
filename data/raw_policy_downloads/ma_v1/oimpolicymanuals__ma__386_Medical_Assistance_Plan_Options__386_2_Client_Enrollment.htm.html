Individuals are enrolled in a Community HealthChoices MCO if they are over 21 and are:

  • Receiving both Medicare and MA
  • Receiving long-term services and supports in the Attendant Care, Independence, COMMCARE, or Aging waivers
  • Receiving services in the OBRA waiver AND determined nursing facility clinically eligible
  • Receiving nursince care in a nursing home by for by MA
  • An Act 150 participant who is dually eligible for Medicare and MA

All other MA eligible indivdiuals are enrolled in HealthChoices MCO unless they fall under any of the conditions in Section 386.12, Fee-For-Service .

For new recipients, an MCO is assigned after eligibility is determined. The recipient though has the ability to change the plan based on their needs (See Section 386.21 Selecting an MCO).

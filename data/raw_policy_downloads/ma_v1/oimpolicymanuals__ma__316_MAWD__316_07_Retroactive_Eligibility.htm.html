316.7 Retroactive Eligibility

The CAO will determine who gets retroactive coverage for MAWD based on the requirements in this section and the following ones:

  • Section 338.3, Retroactive Medical Assistance Eligibility

  • Section 338.3, Retroactive Authorization

The CAO must use the following guidelines to determine who is eligible for MAWD and what the monthly premium payment should be:

  • The MAWD applicant must meet all of the MAWD requirements, including the work requirement for retroactive coverage.

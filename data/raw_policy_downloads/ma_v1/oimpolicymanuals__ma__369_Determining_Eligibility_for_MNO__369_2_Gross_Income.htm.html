369.2 Gross Income

Eligibility for MNO is based on the net income available to the applicant/recipient group after allowable deductions in the combined retroactive/continuing period. This income eligibility period is six calendar months in a row. Figuring out eligibility for MNO begins with the applicant/recipient group's gross income.

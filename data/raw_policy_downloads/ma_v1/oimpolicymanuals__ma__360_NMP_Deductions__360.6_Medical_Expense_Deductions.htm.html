360.6 Medical Expense Deductions

Certain paid and unpaid medical expenses for members of an applicant/recipient group may be deducted from income when determining on eligibility for NMP. Allowable medical expenses can be used to lower net income when the income is above the limit for continuing NMP. Allowable medical expenses must be in the retroactive period.

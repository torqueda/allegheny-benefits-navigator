372.5 Fair Hearing

An individual is entitled to ask for a fair hearing when benefits are lowered or stopped because of a mass change. If the appeal is filed timely, no action to lower or stop benefits is taken until the hearing decision is received. (For more information, see Supplemental Handbook Chapter 870, Appeal and Fair Hearing.)

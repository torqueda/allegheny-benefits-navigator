Appendix B: MAWD Premium Review Intranet Web Page

The Intranet Website http://dhhsintra.dpw.pa.us/MAWD/ was created to provide CAOs with the latest real-time information on premium payments. CAOs can use this website to check premium payment information before taking action on an MAWD budget.

Access information on this file by entering one of the following:

    • e-CIS Recipient ID Number.

    • County/ Record Number.

    • Social Security Number.

Review applicant/recipient information on the Intranet Website:

    • When recipients have questions on premiums due.

    • When an e-CIS alert is received that a premium is overdue. The website must be checked before a notice is sent.

    • Before closing a MAWD budget, after the notice was sent and the appeal period has expired.

    • Before re-opening a MAWD budget to evaluate if premiums are due.

    • To receive the most up to date information on premium payments.

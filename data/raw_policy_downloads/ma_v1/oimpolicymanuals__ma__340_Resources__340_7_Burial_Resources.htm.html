An irrevocable burial reserve is a fund held under contract with a financial institution or a funeral director.ГӮВ ГӮВ No part of the fund may be withdrawn before the individualГўВҖВҷs death. The fund must be used for burial expenses.ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ

None: The CAO will consider life insurance or personal property (such as stock) designated for burial an irrevocable burial reserve if:ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ ГӮВ

  • The funds go toward payment of funeral expenses at the individualГўВҖВҷs death.ГӮВ ГӮВ

None: Pennsylvania law requires the contract to include a clause allowing an individual to withdraw his money if he moves out of state.ГӮВ ГӮВ The CAO should consider the funds irrevocable if it meets all other requirements.ГӮВ ГӮВ
